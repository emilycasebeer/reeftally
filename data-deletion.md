# Reef Tally — Data Deletion

  Reef Tally does not require an account. Your data is linked to an anonymous ID stored on your device.

## How to delete your data

  1. Open the Reef Tally app
  2. Tap the **Profile** tab
  3. Tap **Reset All Data**

  This immediately and permanently deletes all your bag history from our servers.

## Reinstalling the app

  Uninstalling Reef Tally also severs the link between your device and any previously submitted data.
